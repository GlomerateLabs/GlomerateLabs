Virtual Classroom

A VLE system for everyone

Technology has advanced and had progressed in many ways like, social media, mobile, cloud and analytics. School and technology have never really bonded and co-existed.

The idea of a virtual classroom has come and gone but the idea has never take off. With digital becoming more readily available.

Virtual Classroom is a modular virtual learing enviroment, so it can change and adapt depending on any schools requirements. This all-in-one software allows teacher to set homework, questionnaires and reminders for up coming trips and tests. Students can then login to ask questions and upload homework and do online tests. Teachers will also have the option to upload the lessson as the software will conect via Bluetooth to a webcam. When connecting the teacher will be promoted to enter their username and password then the lesson title, the lesson will begin and so does the recording. Once the lesson ends the teacher then can add the supporting files/resources to the video.

Built as a web application with a simplistic user interface, makes it useable to any teacher with out any special training. Schools can simply subscibe with a pay as you go model, so the more resources the school uses the more money they pay, making it affordale to the schools.

    Redefining group discussions

    Blackboards and whiteboards have been used to for the past centuary to share and draw ideas, but it was almost impossible to save the work and send to someone else.

    Digitalising the whiteboard will greatly increase productivity in groups allowing for easiyer interaction and sharing of notes.

    Our idea is to digitalise the whiteboard so it can recognise input and display it on screen, while working on most applications such as Microsoft Powerpoint and Adobe Acrobat.

    This will allow people to annotate on documents and other files allowing them to collaboratively have a group discussion and save all the annotations made, which can later be easily redistributed and edited.

    The idea is to have a infrared sensor with Bluetooth chip which will be used to send signals to the computer. The infrared sensor will sense when the infrared pen is turned on and will record the coordinate of the point this will then be sent to the computer via bluetooth where is will be plotted on the application for the user to see.

    The sensor will know the boundaries on the grid as when the application will be loaded up the the use will be asked to put the pointer on all four corners. This will allow the computer to and sensor to create a grid based on the four extremes.

    ZaZen

    Takeing you anywhere

    True meditation is when you are transprted to a place of comfort and tanquility. For many people that place can vary, from forests to space or to the city center.

    Music and visuals can help relax the user, reducting stress and anxiety allowing them to feel energised and be more productive.

    Using VR and 3D surround sound on mobile devices we can allow people to effortlessly relax in their own home and go somewhere new. Targeting teenages and young adults who have a stressful lives we beilive that this is be a solution as it is cheap and easily accesable by anyone. Simply pick a video and plug in headphones and go anywhere.

    A simple app that can be loaded on any VR/AR comatible phone within seconds and the easy to use interface allows for stress free interaction.

    Designed to reduce the £13.5 billion the NHS currently spends on stress and mental health related illnesses, which is 11 % of the total budget. Mental health impacting 1 in 4 people in the UK.
